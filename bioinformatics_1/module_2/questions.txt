Q. The position of the E. coli genome at which the skew attains a minimum value is most likely near which of the following?
A. the origin of replication

Q. True or False: Deamination is more likely to occur when DNA is single-stranded than when it is double-stranded.
A. True

Q. The position of the E. coli genome at which the skew attains a maximum value is most likely near which of the following?
A. the replication terminus

Q. Consecutive Okazaki fragments are sewn together by which of the following?
A. DNA ligase
